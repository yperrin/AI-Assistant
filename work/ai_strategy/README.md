# AI Deployment Strategy - Personal Workspace

This folder acts as your **personal overlay** for the company's AI deployment strategy.

## Guidelines:
- **Do NOT** paste sensitive company code or proprietary architectural diagrams here.
- **DO** track your personal goals, daily reflections, meeting summaries, and rough drafts.
- When drafting content meant for the team, use `rough_drafts.md` as a scratchpad, then port it to the official company wiki/repo.

## Recommended Files:
- `personal_log.md` - Your private journal on the project's progress and challenges.
- `rough_drafts.md` - Sandbox for brainstorming with agents.
- `focus_tasks.md` - Your private to-do list.
