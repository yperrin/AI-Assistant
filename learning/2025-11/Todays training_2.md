- **Find the Scarcity:** Identify the new bottleneck (e.g., sequencing or implementation) in a world of abundant intelligence [01:35 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=95).

- **Context & Constraints:** Show value by understanding the unique, non-transferable elements of a situation and judging what is *possible* to build *now* [02:55 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=175), [04:11 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=251).

- **Focus & Sequencing:** Know what **not** to do (deprioritization) [06:16 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=376) and thin-slice your value to deliver quick wins that earn trust and momentum (sequencing) [04:58 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=298).

- **Social & Responsibility:** Map the decision-makers (coalition) [09:04 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=544) and carry ownership by being accountable for the consequences of your decisions [10:34 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=634).

- **Transparency & Systems:** Be transparent about your reasoning and trade-offs [11:18 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=678), and encode your best judgment into lasting organizational systems (playbooks) [12:46 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O_VL5clgN_I&t=766).
