### The Core Philosophy: Jevons Paradox

### The 6 Strategic Unlocks

1. **Iterate at High Speeds:** Move from 2–4 "product bets" per year to hundreds. Tools like [Cursor](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU) already allow for parallel autonomous agents, compressing months of work into days [04:53 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU&t=293).

1. **Unlock "Domain Expert" Builders:** The "translation layer" between knowing a problem and coding a solution is disappearing. AI allows doctors, teachers, and logistics managers to build production-quality tools directly [08:17 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU&t=497).

1. **Quality as a Default:** High-level testing, documentation, and security review—often skipped due to cost—are now "agent-verifiable" table stakes. Differentiation will move from "working code" to "amazing product experience" [10:10 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU&t=610).

1. **Every Company is a Platform:** Cheap, AI-driven integrations mean every system is now fundamentally open. Leaders must socialize high-level platform strategy down to individual contributors [12:30 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU&t=750).

1. **Market for Ambition:** CFOs must rethink ROI. A $10M market that wasn't worth a $3M engineering spend now becomes highly viable when execution costs drop 10x [13:42 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU&t=822).

1. **Move at the Speed of Insight:** The organization must "default to code." When a customer insight is found, it should be acted upon immediately rather than getting stuck in manual documentation or leadership hierarchies [15:34 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-giatW9mYU&t=934).
