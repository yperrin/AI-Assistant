### **Key Skills for the AI Era**

- **Taste vs. Conviction:** While *taste* is the ability to recognize what is "good," **conviction** is the willingness to act and ship before getting external validation [08:11 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zCW-k5fFRgQ&t=491).

- **Speed of Control:** Rather than just "span of control" (managing many agents), the focus is on **attention sequencing**—the ability to quickly triage information and make high-quality editorial decisions [13:03 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zCW-k5fFRgQ&t=783).

- **Judgment Density:** The capacity for relevant pattern recognition that allows a person to understand what constitutes a "good" decision in changing conditions [28:59 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zCW-k5fFRgQ&t=1739).
