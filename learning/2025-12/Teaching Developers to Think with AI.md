### Key Strategies for AI Learning:

- **Trust but Verify:** Learners are shown early on that AI can be "confidently wrong" (e.g., failing at simple loop logic) to prevent over-reliance and encourage critical thinking.

- **Prompting as Coding:** The framework teaches that writing a detailed prompt is as rigorous as writing a coding exercise, helping developers understand the effort required for high-quality output.

- **AI as a Research Tool:** Instead of just generating code, learners use AI to explain their own logic, add comments, and research new C# topics using specific personas and response styles.

- **Unit Test Generation:** Learners practice AI-assisted coding by generating unit tests, which requires them to clearly describe the behavior and interface of the code they are testing.
