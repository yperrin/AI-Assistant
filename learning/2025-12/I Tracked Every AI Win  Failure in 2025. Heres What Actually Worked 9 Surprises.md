### **Shifts in Strategy & Labor**

- **The "Messy Middle" is the Opportunity:** There is immense value in the layer between raw model outputs and end-user applications [07:51 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVZMc-i5EEs&t=471). This includes routing intent, handling exceptions, and transforming data into structured formats [08:32 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVZMc-i5EEs&t=512).

- **System Design Over AI Expertise:** Nate noted that individuals who focus on **system design** (using templates, validators, and retries) often out-execute traditional development teams who "worship" specific models [04:42 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVZMc-i5EEs&t=282).

- **Creative Problem Solving:** The labor market is increasingly rewarding "liberal arts" instincts—creative people who use AI to become technical and technical people who use it to express creativity [11:55 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVZMc-i5EEs&t=715).
