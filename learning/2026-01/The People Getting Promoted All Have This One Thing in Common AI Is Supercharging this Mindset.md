### **Core Concepts**

- **The Disappearing Ladder:** Entry-level hiring has collapsed because AI now performs the "training tasks" (summarizing, data cleaning, drafting) that used to teach newcomers how to operate [00:46 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HZ9iL_lFYgQ&t=46).

- **High Agency & Locus of Control:** High agency is defined by an **internal locus of control**—the belief that you control your outcomes. High-agency individuals view obstacles as "skill issues" that can be resolved rather than external barriers [03:20 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HZ9iL_lFYgQ&t=200).

- **AI as an Equalizer:** AI acts as a "jet engine" for agency, allowing individuals to bypass traditional gatekeepers (pedigree, expensive education) and build massive value with minimal resources [06:06 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HZ9iL_lFYgQ&t=366).

### **The High Agency Framework**

- **The "Say-Do" Ratio:** Success is correlated with collapsing the distance between saying you'll do something and actually doing it. This means starting before you feel "ready" and using AI to fill immediate skill gaps [12:37 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HZ9iL_lFYgQ&t=757).

- **Value Creation:** High-agency people are obsessed with pushing value into the world. They believe the world is "bendable" if you contribute enough and expand your locus of control [18:22 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HZ9iL_lFYgQ&t=1102).

- **AI Native Thinking:** Passive users treat AI as an answer engine, while high-agency users treat it as an extension of their own capacity to prototype, research, and execute at scale [14:45 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HZ9iL_lFYgQ&t=885).
