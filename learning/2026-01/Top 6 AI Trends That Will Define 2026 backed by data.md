- **Trend 1: Models Are Commodities** – The performance gap between top AI models (Gemini, GPT, Claude) is shrinking, and costs are plummeting [00:51 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=51). Competition is shifting from raw power to how well these models are integrated into existing apps and workflows [02:28 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=148).

- **Trend 2: Year of Workflows, Not Agents** – While fully autonomous "agents" are the long-term goal, 2026 is about **AI Workflows** [03:20 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=200). Organizations are seeing more success using "agent-light" systems (like custom GPTs) to automate specific, repeatable parts of a process rather than giving AI full autonomy [04:33 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=273).

- **Trend 3: The End of the Technical Divide** – AI is acting as a massive equalizer. Non-technical employees in sales and marketing are now using AI to write code, clean data, and build dashboards—tasks they previously had to outsource to specialist teams [06:14 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=374).

- **Trend 4: Context > Prompting** – Precise "prompt engineering" is becoming less critical as models get better at understanding vague intent. The new priority is **Context** [07:59 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=479). Success now depends on your file management and whether the AI has access to your specific data, emails, and goals [08:30 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=510).

- **Trend 5: Advertising in Chatbots** – Ads are coming to AI platforms like ChatGPT in 2026 [09:16 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=556). This is expected to take the form of display banners rather than integrated answers, and it's seen as necessary to fund free access to powerful models for those who can't afford subscriptions [10:24 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=624).

- **Trend 6: From Chatbots to Robots** – AI is moving into the physical world via autonomous taxis (Waymo) and warehouse robots [11:01 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=661). Physical assets are becoming "software endpoints" that actually improve over time through updates, similar to how smartphones function [11:52 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B23W1gRT9eY&t=712).
