### Key Points

- **Definition of Ethereal UI:** Interfaces that "spawn from nothing" for a single user and a single purpose. A common example is an AI-generated table or graph in [ChatGPT](https://medium.com/plans?dimension=post_audio_button&postId=dac3a9e9f8b9) or [Gemini](https://medium.com/plans?dimension=post_audio_button&postId=dac3a9e9f8b9) that exists only for that specific interaction.

- **The AI Impact:** AI is transitioning from a tool used by designers to a creator of interfaces. This removes the need for designers to manually craft every basic component like buttons or layout grids for standard data display.

- **The Shift in Design Roles:** While AI currently produces "6/10 quality" work, the author predicts a major pivot for designers over the next decade. Designers will move away from "pretty buttons" and color palettes to focus on:

	- **Elevated Experience Processes:** Complex customer journeys and high-level strategy.

	- **Ethical AI Design:** Designing the logic, outputs, and ethical boundaries of machine learning flows.

	- **Agentic Workflows:** Moving toward systems handled via voice or chat where traditional UIs are no longer the primary interface.

- **Evolution, Not Extinction:** The author argues that UI design isn't dying but evolving, much like how washing machines freed people from manual labor to focus on more complex tasks.

### Key Points:

- **The Concept of Ethereal UI**: Unlike traditional platforms like Facebook or Instagram that serve millions with one interface, ethereal UIs are generated from scratch for one specific user and purpose, then discarded.

- **Driven by Multi-modal LLMs**: This shift is made possible by AI models that can generate components like tables or graphs on demand, effectively acting as the designer and developer in real-time.

- **Shift in Design Focus**: As AI takes over basic tasks like creating "pretty buttons" or layout grids, UI designers will need to pivot toward higher-level strategy, complex customer journeys, and ethical AI design.

- **Evolution of the Role**: The author compares this transition to how washing machines automated manual labor, allowing people to focus on more complex tasks; UI design is not dying but evolving into a more "elevated" discipline.

- **Future Outlook**: While AI quality is currently rated around "6/10," designers are encouraged to prepare for a significant industry shift over the next decade.

- **Ethical and Agentic Needs**: Commenters on the article highlight that designers will still be essential for defining the ethical boundaries of AI flows and managing "agentic" systems that function through voice or chat.

### **Prompt-to-Prototype & Live Site Engines**

- **[Figma AI (Figma Make)](https://www.builder.io/blog/ai-figma)**: A prompt-to-prototype engine that generates interactive UI flows, component states, and user journeys from text descriptions. It also includes **Figma Sites**, which can transform these designs directly into live, responsive websites with animations and interactions.

- **[Google Stitch](https://www.uxdesigninstitute.com/blog/user-interface-ui-design-tools/)**: Currently in beta, this tool specializes in creating cross-platform interfaces (Android, ChromeOS, and Web) within the **Material Design** ecosystem, ensuring consistent UI systems across diverse environments.

- **[Framer AI](https://emergent.sh/learn/best-ai-tools-for-ui-design)**: This platform transforms text prompts into complete web page layouts, including hero sections, pricing blocks, and full component systems that are immediately publish-ready.

- **[Galileo AI](https://emergent.sh/learn/best-ai-tools-for-ui-design)**: A "Figma-first" generator known for producing high-fidelity, aesthetic UI screens for SaaS dashboards and mobile apps that can be immediately edited as Figma frames.

### **Design-to-Code & Full-Stack Generation**

- **[Emergent](https://emergent.sh/learn/best-ai-tools-for-ui-design)**: An AI-native "vibe coding" platform. It uses multi-agent architecture to generate UI, backend, and deployment simultaneously from conversational prompts, providing clean React, Next.js, or Expo code.

- **[Builder.io Fusion](https://www.builder.io/blog/ai-figma)**: Instead of static mockups, Fusion connects Figma designs directly to your production codebase and design system, allowing you to design with **real data** and commit changes via a standard Git workflow.

- **[Ux Pilot](https://uxpilot.ai/)**: Focuses on the "logic" of the ethereal UI by generating wireframes, UX flows, and usability insights based on user behavior modeling before high-fidelity visuals are even created.

### **Specialized Generative Features**

- **[Adobe Firefly](https://www.builder.io/blog/best-ai-tools-for-designers)**: Deeply integrated into Photoshop and Illustrator, providing commercially safe "Generative Fill" and background removal tools that speed up asset creation for unique interfaces.

- **[Uizard](https://www.uxdesigninstitute.com/blog/user-interface-ui-design-tools/)**: Features a "Screenshot-to-UI" scanner that transforms images or hand-drawn sketches into editable digital layouts.

- **[Visily](https://www.uxdesigninstitute.com/blog/user-interface-ui-design-tools/)**: Offers "Instant Restyling," allowing designers to apply completely new visual themes and branding to entire application screens with a single click.
