### The Core Shift

### 8 Habits to Break for "AI-Native" Work

1. **The Permission Loop:** Stop waiting for buy-in. The time spent on email threads for approval often exceeds the time needed to just build the prototype. [11:25 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=685)

1. **Polish as Procrastination:** Avoid spending 80% of your time on the final 20% of quality. "Shipping ugly" allows you to test ideas against reality sooner. [13:53 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=833)

1. **Meetings as a Default:** Replace alignment meetings with product demos. If six people meet for an hour, that’s six hours of lost execution time—enough to build a rough version of the idea. [15:14 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=914)

1. **Structured Waiting:** Don't let your momentum depend on someone else's calendar. Assume the answer is "yes" or make a provisional decision and keep moving. [17:13 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=1033)

1. **Over-Planning:** Prediction is now more "expensive" than doing. Cut your planning time by up to 90% and let the results of prototyping inform your strategy. [18:42 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=1122)

1. **The Deck instead of the Demo:** Stop building "walking around decks." Build a working prototype to show stakeholders what is actually possible. [20:05 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=1205)

1. **Consensus before Action:** Let results create alignment. Trying something and showing the data is more persuasive than trying to get everyone to agree beforehand. [21:46 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=1306)

1. **Hoarding until Ready:** Show your work while it's raw. Early feedback prevents you from investing too deeply in a direction that might be incorrect. [23:13 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=1393)

### The New Bottlenecks

- **Clarity:** Knowing exactly what is worth building for the customer. [05:34 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=334)

- **Ambition:** Having the courage to take "50 swings a year" rather than just a few safe bets. [07:06 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=426)

- **Distribution:** The ability to actually get products into users' hands (e.g., [Cognition's partnership with Infosys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI)). [08:03 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=483)

- **Relationships:** Durable professional trust that cannot be "vibe-coded." [09:01 Opens in a new window](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hpDC29JdgjI&t=541)
