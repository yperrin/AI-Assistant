### **Core Thesis: The Rise of Intent Engineering**

- **Prompt Engineering ****[05:01 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=301)****:** Individual, session-based skill (Instruction).

- **Context Engineering ****[05:26 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=326)****:** Wiring up RAG pipelines and structuring knowledge (Information).

- **Intent Engineering ****[06:20 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=380)****:** Encoding organizational purpose and structured actionable parameters so agents "want" what the company wants (Direction).

### **The Klarna Cautionary Tale**

- **The Problem:** The AI optimized for "resolution speed" (measurable) rather than "customer relationship quality" (unmeasured intent) [02:11 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=131).

- **The Lesson:** Human agents intuitively know when to bend policies or spend extra time; AI agents lack this "organizational intent" unless it is explicitly engineered into them [02:50 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=170).

### **The Three Layers of AI Maturity**

1. **Unified Context Infrastructure ****[11:19 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=679)****:** Moving away from "Shadow Agents" toward standardized protocols like the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** [12:17 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=737).

1. **Coherent AI Worker Toolkit ****[13:57 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=837)****:** Shifting from individual AI activity to organizational AI fluency—where infrastructure makes the entire team better [14:31 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=871).

1. **Intent Engineering Proper ****[16:17 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=977)****:** Translating human-readable OKRs into machine-actionable objectives, delegation frameworks, and decision boundaries [18:00 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=1080).

### **Future Outlook**

- **New Role:** The video predicts the rise of the **AI Workflow Architect** [23:39 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=1419), a role sitting between engineering, operations, and strategy.

- **The Bottleneck:** Models (GPT-5, Gemini 3, etc.) are no longer the bottleneck; the race is now an "intent race" to build the best organizational infrastructure [26:04 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QWzLPn164w0&t=1564).
