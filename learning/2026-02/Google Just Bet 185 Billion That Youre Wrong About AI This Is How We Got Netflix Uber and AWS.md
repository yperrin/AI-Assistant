### **The Core Thesis: From Training to Inference**

- **The Shift:** We are moving from the "training phase" (front-loaded, bursty compute) to the "inference phase" (continuous, massive token burn) [11:44 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCgdpbEvNVA&t=704).

- **The Agent Multiplier:** While a human using a chatbot creates modest workloads, an **AI agent** (coding, auditing, or managing supply chains) generates **1,000x** the workload, running 24/7 [12:04 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCgdpbEvNVA&t=724).

- **Vertical Integration:** Unlike previous "dumb pipe" infrastructure (railroads or fiber), AI infra is vertically integrated. Companies aren't just selling bandwidth; they are selling **intelligence/cognitive capability** [09:59 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCgdpbEvNVA&t=599).

- **The SAS Apocalypse:** A simple markdown file (Claude's legal agent) wiped billions off enterprise software market caps, proving that agents are now powerful enough to restructure entire industries [03:01 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCgdpbEvNVA&t=181).

- **Massive Capex:** The "Big Five" tech companies are projected to spend nearly **$700 billion** on AI infrastructure in 2026 alone [05:40 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCgdpbEvNVA&t=340).

- **Infrastructure Inversion Window:** History shows that those who build the platform (like Amazon with AWS) capture the economics, while those who wait become permanent "tenants" paying high margins [14:12 Opens in a new window ](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCgdpbEvNVA&t=852).
